FAGO

Fargo is a soulful, fire-kissed grains-and-protein dish infused with smoky pepppers,roasted herbs,and hint of citrus.Served with sweet plantain bites and a silky, spiced sauce for dipping and drizzling.

Ingredients

  • Grains(millet,rice,or sorghum)
  • Pepper
  • Herbs
  • Fish/Meat
  • Plantain/Yam or Nuts
  • Pepper Oil

Cooking Instruction

  1. Fago is cooked over open fire
  2. Grains are toasted in a wide clay or iron pot until they release a nutty aroma.
  3. water or broth is added slowly,letting steam rise.
  4. A sauce of grounded fire roasted Peppers,onions,garlic,and local herbs is poured over the grains and stirred
  5. The protiens and Plantain/yam/roasted nuts are added and left to cook for 20mins.

Note: Fago isn't just food - it's a performance of community, ancestry and flavour.
